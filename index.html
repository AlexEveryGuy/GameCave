It Starts Here

A little about me, and why this site exists...

I’ve learned to love gaming from a different perspective.

I’ve always been infatuated and mesmerized by video games. They’ve often been a window for me to enter into worlds that either I was too afraid to in real life, or simply couldn’t. Gaming became somewhat of an addiction at one point, and I had to take a hard look and realize that it was a placeholder for dealing with my own anxiety and difficulties in life. Yet, with changes in my life outlook, my new marriage, and my changing surroundings (friends, mentors and family) I’ve learned to love gaming from a different perspective.
